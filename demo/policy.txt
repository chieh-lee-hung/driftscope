PICNIC REFUND POLICY
====================
Version: v1.0
Updated: 2026-03-23

STANDARD DAMAGE POLICY
-----------------------
All refund requests for damaged or spoiled items are automatically
approved. No additional verification required.

Tool workflow:
  search_policy → check_order → process_refund

Eligible: damaged packaging, spoiled products, missing items.
Turnaround: 3–5 business days after approval.
